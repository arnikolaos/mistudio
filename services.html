Our Services

Concept Design


We specialize in early-stage design exploration, transforming ideas into conceptual frameworks that inspire. Using advanced parametric tools, 3D modeling, and hybrid workflows, we develop massing studies, program explorations, natural light simulation and parti concepts that are both innovative and adaptable. This process allows us to explore bold ideas quickly while maintaining the flexibility to refine or shift directions as needed. Whether you're a client envisioning your dream project or an architect seeking fresh design insights, our concept design services are tailored to meet your needs.



Architectural Design


Our architectural design services blend creativity and technical expertise to deliver visionary solutions. Starting with concept exploration and hybrid sketching, we work iteratively to refine designs from bold ideas to detailed realities. Combining parametric tools with traditional methods, we craft spaces that harmonize functionality, aesthetics, and sustainability. From initial massing to final detailing, every element is tailored to meet your project's unique goals. We design parametric facades for both new and renovated buildings, enhancing energy efficiency, light optimization, and aesthetic appeal. For renovation projects, we integrate modern parametric solutions with existing structures, ensuring seamless design continuity. We also conduct natural light experiments and simulations, optimizing sunlight to improve spatial quality, energy efficiency, and overall ambiance in every project.



Interior Design


Our interior design services blend creativity, functionality, and diverse styles, including biophilic design and modern minimalism. We focus on every detail, from spatial planning to materials, tailoring each design to our clients’ needs. With our unique 3D visualization expertise, we refine every element for perfection. Committed to innovation and sustainability, we create beautiful, functional, and inspiring interiors suited to various styles.


Architectural Stills


We create high-quality architectural visualization stills that capture the essence of your design, offering an unparalleled way to showcase spaces in a realistic and artistic manner. Perfect for architectural studios, interior designers, real estate professionals, and clients, these detailed images bring your vision to life, making it possible to experience and present spaces with precision and elegance.


Cinematic Videos


Our 3D cinematic videos showcase the true scale and elegance of your designs through motion and emotion, offering an immersive experience that static images cannot. To enhance realism, we integrate advanced VFX techniques, blending 3D elements with real footage when needed. This fusion elevates the storytelling and impact of your project, leaving a lasting impression.


3D Walkthroughs


Our walkthroughs offer a dynamic exploration of your spaces, catering to both interactive and cinematic needs. Whether navigating freely through an interactive 3D environment or enjoying a guided, cinematic tour, these walkthroughs provide a comprehensive understanding of spatial layouts and interior designs. They’re designed to engage and inform clients, enhancing the presentation of your vision.


Parametric Design & Conceptual Support


We offer specialized services in parametric design and concept development, providing tailored solutions for architects, designers, and creative studios. Using advanced computational tools and 3D workflows, we assist in: Developing innovative massing studies and program explorations. Generating adaptive parametric models for flexibility and precision. Refining early-stage design concepts to align with project goals. Whether you need a fresh perspective, technical modeling expertise, or support integrating parametric approaches into your process, we’re here to elevate your designs.


Advanced Visualization R&D


Push the boundaries of architectural representation with our experimental visualization services. We combine VFX, hybrid 3D sketches, and innovative storytelling techniques to create impactful, one-of-a-kind visualizations that elevate your project. From conceptual visual storytelling for competitions to futuristic renders and videos that blend artistic freedom with styles like pencil, oil, cartoon, cyberpunk, and retro, we provide immersive and dynamic representations. We also offer interactive visualization tools that integrate AR/VR and real-time simulations, bringing your ideas to life in unique and exciting ways.


Collaborative Design Partnerships


We offer collaborative design partnerships to enhance both the creative and technical aspects of your projects. From brainstorming to parametric modeling, visualization, and real-time feedback, we seamlessly integrate with your team. Our expertise in hybrid 3D sketching and computational design ensures efficient development and refinement, helping you elevate your vision and achieve the best outcome.
