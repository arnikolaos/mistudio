Our Story


MI Architects was founded by Arvanitis Nikolaos, a visionary architect and 3D artist with a passion for creating breathtaking visual experiences. From a young age, Nikolaos had a deep interest in the fusion of art and architecture, leading to the establishment of MI Architects, a studio dedicated to transforming bold ideas into stunning realities.

With a portfolio that spans across multiple genres, including architectural stills, cinematic videos, AR/VR, and immersive walkthroughs, exterior & interior design, MI Architects is committed to pushing the boundaries of visual storytelling in the architectural world.

At MI Architects, we believe in the power of collaboration, innovation, and the pursuit of excellence. Our team is driven by a shared mission to take our clients' visions and turn them into visuals and realities that inspire, engage, and captivate.
