Orokami Villa

Orokami Villa おろ神 is a rare sanctuary envisioned atop the cliffs above one of the world’s most celebrated beaches in Lefkada — believed by many scholars to be the true Ithaca of Homer’s Odyssey. Hovering between mountain (oros in Greek) and spirit (kami in Japanese), its name captures the architectural essence: a fusion of grounded earth and quiet divinity. This expertly designed villa is a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to own a fully realized architectural vision — now available off-plan.

Thoughtfully composed and meticulously detailed, the design aligns every space to the sea, offering 270-degree unobstructed views — from two en suite bedrooms, a semi-outdoor lounge, an open kitchen, and a sculptural amphitheater-style concrete bench lounge, to the iconic infinity pool, a guests’ bathroom, a beautiful mezzanine with balcony, and expansive infinity rooftop terraces with breathtaking 360-degree vistas of the sea horizon and sloping terrain.

A private back terrace with a small reflective pool and curated native gardens complete this elemental retreat. Circular openings, subterranean gestures, and sculpted lines bring light, shadow, and silence into harmony. Continuous microcement surfaces flow across built-in beds, walls, and furnishings — a tactile expression of calm and cohesion. Orokami is more than a home — it is an architectural sanctuary in the making, offered as a turnkey vision, ready to be brought to life and to welcome its first spirit.

Zathera Villa

Zathera Villa rises gracefully from the terraced slopes of a tranquil Ionian landscape, embodying an architectural dialogue between light, earth, and eternity. Centered around a majestic olive tree that serves as the inspiration and spatial axis of the design, the villa unfolds as a sculptural reflection on time, memory, and the elemental beauty of nature. Its monolithic form is carefully anchored into the terrain, balancing monumentality with intimacy through clean lines, thoughtful voids, and sheltered outdoor spaces that frame breathtaking views from mountain to sea.

Crafted with tactile tsimentokonia plaster blended with local sand, natural stone, warm wood accents, and expansive glass, the materials root the home deeply in place while dissolving boundaries between interior and landscape. The open-plan living spaces flow seamlessly, embracing the olive tree and a surrounding pool that provides both meditation and natural cooling. Two secluded bedrooms with en-suite baths, a minimal floating staircase, and subtle lighting complete a residence designed to age gracefully and offer timeless Mediterranean luxury.

Zathera Villa has been featured in leading publications including Compasses Magazine (Dubai), Archilovers, and Archetype.gr, recognizing its architectural significance and design excellence. Delivered turnkey—from detailed architectural plans to a fully realized sanctuary—Zathera offers discerning buyers a rare opportunity to own a thoughtful, poetic home that celebrates slowness, connection to place, and enduring material truth.
